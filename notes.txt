#DRUM MACHINE
1. Outer container with id="drum-machine"

2. Element id="display" within #drum-machine

3. 9 clickable drum pad .drum-pad elements inside #drum-machine (Q, W, E, A, S, D, Z, X, C)

4. There should be and HTML5 audio element within each .drum-pad with a src pointing to the audio clip and class="clip" and id corresponding to the innerText of its parent .drum-pad

5., 6. .drum-pad onClick or keydown must trigger the audio element

7. when a .drum-pad is triggered, the #display innerText should show the name of the audio clip
